Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

CBT is a structured therapy approach that studies the connection between thoughts,
feelings, body sensations, and behaviors. A common CBT exercise is to identify
automatic thoughts, evaluate evidence for and against them, and develop a more
balanced thought.

Example: If a student thinks "I will fail every exam", CBT would ask what evidence
supports that thought, what evidence does not support it, and what a realistic
alternative thought might be.
